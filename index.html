Who I Am

A Clinician Built for Complex Realities

I am a Consultant Psychiatrist with a distinguished career spanning federal neuropsychiatric hospitals, private rehabilitation centres, state medical facilities, and academic institutions across Enugu and Anambra States.

My practice integrates general adult psychiatry, addiction medicine, and liaison psychiatry. As the institutional founder of the Telepsychiatry programme at Neuropsychiatric Hospital Nawfia, I pioneered remote psychiatric services in Anambra State.

This platform serves as a professional information resource and institutional liaison point — facilitating referrals between clinicians, collaboration between institutions, and advocacy for accessible mental health care across South-East Nigeria. It is not intended for direct patient solicitation.

Vision & Mission

Advancing Telepsychiatry & Telemedicine in Nigeria

This platform is more than a professional profile. It is a statement of intent — a demonstration that technology and psychiatry can meet to close the mental health treatment gap across Nigeria and beyond.

The Vision

“A Nigeria where geography is no longer a barrier to quality mental health care — where every patient, from the rural community to the urban centre, can access a consultant psychiatrist through their phone.”

— Dr. Olisa Montfort Uche

📱 Remote Consultations

Pioneering real-time video and audio psychiatric consultations, enabling patients in underserved communities across Anambra and Enugu States to access specialist care without the burden of long-distance travel.

🏠 Institutional Telepsychiatry

As founder of the Telepsychiatry programme at Neuropsychiatric Hospital Nawfia, Dr. Olisa has demonstrated that telemedicine can be embedded into existing hospital infrastructure — a replicable model for facilities across Nigeria.

🌐 Expanding the Reach

The development of this digital platform is itself an expression of that mission — designed and built by Dr. Olisa to demonstrate how technology skills, clinical expertise, and psychiatric care can integrate to serve more people, more efficiently.

🏠 For Hospital Administrators & CMDs

Is your facility underserved by psychiatric services? Telepsychiatry can bridge that gap — without the cost and complexity of a full in-house unit.

Dr. Olisa is available to advise on the design, implementation, and clinical governance of telepsychiatry programmes within existing hospital infrastructure — drawing on the replicable model established at Neuropsychiatric Hospital Nawfia.

📋 For Clinicians & Referring Practitioners

Managing a patient with complex psychiatric needs in a setting without specialist cover? Telepsychiatry enables real-time specialist-to-specialist consultation across any distance.
